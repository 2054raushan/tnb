Our Story

A kitchen that never left the coal behind

Thali & Beyond began in 2015 as a single clay tandoor set up by an Indian family who had made Muscat home. Today the fire is bigger, the dining room quieter, and the recipes exactly the same — nothing here is shortcut. Our spice blends are stone-ground each morning, our marinades rest overnight, and every biryani is sealed and slow-cooked in dum style, the way it was taught to our head chef by his grandmother in Lucknow — served here with an Omani welcome, from Arabic coffee to dates on arrival.
